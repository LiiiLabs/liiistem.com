About Liii Network

Empowering Scientific Writing in the AI Era

Our Mission
    Liii Network Technology Co., Ltd. is dedicated to revolutionizing scientific and technical writing through AI-powered tools and innovative software solutions. We bridge the gap between traditional LaTeX complexity and modern user expectations.
What We Do
  • Develop Liii STEM Suite: An AI-integrated scientific writing platform that makes professional document creation accessible to everyone
  • Maintain Open Source Projects: Lead the development of Mogan Editor and contribute to the GNU TeXmacs ecosystem
  • Create Writing Tools: Build features like Magic Paste, intelligent formula editing, and automated referencing systems

Section Numbering and Referencing in Liii STEM ​

I. Feature Introduction ​

This feature provides automatic numbering and cross-referencing for sections with bidirectional linking. The example below uses "subsection" level.

II. Instructions ​

STEP 1. Insert Section ​

  • Click sequentially in the toolbar: Insert -> Section -> Subsection (as shown in the image below)

Note:

  • Generally, the system will automatically number your section after insertion. If you find it's not numbered, you can place the cursor on the section and click the Ⅳ style button on the toolbar to implement it manually.
  • You can also quickly change the section level in the toolbar. (as shown in the image below)

STEP 2. Section Naming ​

  • Place the cursor at the end of the section and ensure the input method is in English mode
  • Click \, type label, (or use the shortcut: ctrl+!), press Enter
  • Enter your section name, such as "section3-1", press Enter (as shown in the image below)

STEP 3. Establish Bidirectional Link ​

  • At the place where you need to reference this section
  • Click \, type ref, press Enter
  • Enter the section name you just named again, press Enter (as shown in the image below)

Welcome to the Template Center! ​

Welcome to the Liii STEM Template Center! Here you'll find carefully crafted .tmu document templates that provide professional support for your document creation. We offer a rich collection of educational resource templates, from exam papers to beautiful slides, helping you create high-quality teaching content.

Key Features ​

  • Document Templates: Provides various types of .tmu document templates, including standardized exam papers, academic papers, lesson plan designs, and other professional templates.
  • Slide Templates: Carefully designed university-themed PPT templates that support rapid creation of professional presentations.
  • Member Customization: Exclusive customization service for members, allowing custom templates based on teaching needs. See Fruit Membership for details.

Usage Guide ​

  1. Browse template categories and select the desired document or slide template
  2. Click preview to view template details and examples
  3. Download the .tmu template file and open it with Liii STEM
  4. Modify and adjust content according to your needs

For technical support, please see Contact Support Team, and we will be happy to serve you.
